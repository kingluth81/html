Anime Recommendations: Jojo's Bizarre Adventure
Genre Adventure
Seasons Six

Jojo's Bizarre Adeventure may be classified as an Adventure anime but it showcases so many other genres as well:

  • Comedy
  • Romance
  • Horror

The presence of these various genres has undoubtedly contributed to the huge success of the show because each episode is a thrilled adventure. Since nothing is off limits when it comes to this anime, it's no chore to stay invested in the unpredictable and twisted plot. This anime has a total of five completed seasons so far with the sixth season currently in development. The continuity of this anime has given viewers the opportunity to build connections to the unique characters and watch the storyline grow.

Here is the watch order of the show:

  1. Phantom Blood
  2. Battle Tendency
  3. Stardust Crusaders
  4. Diamond is Unbreakable
  5. Golden Wind
  6. Stone Ocean
