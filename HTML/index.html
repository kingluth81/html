About Me

If my friends were asked to describe me in three words, they would describe me as
kind, smart, and funny. I always try to put others before myself and I'm always
available to lend an ear or a shoulder to the poeple in my life. My friends believe
I am an unnaturally calm person, because I manage to remain calm under pressure and
in very stressful situations. In other words, I like to stay positive and try not
to dwell on my mistakes, but instead grow from them. I'm sure my humour is not for
everyone but for anyone, like my friends, who shares the same sense of homour as
myself, you know that timing is everything.

My ethnicity is Nigerian from both my mom and dad.
I was born in Minneapolis, Minnesota which makes me a dual Tri-citizen with American, Nigerian, and Canadian citizenship
I've been told that I am a carbon copy of my mom. I have black curly hair,
browm eyes, and a round nose. In terms
of my personality, however, I am a mixture of my mom and dad.
I am very stubborn, and can be very hostile when provoked. Being Nigerian, I love jollof rice
(yes, Nigerian Jollof rice is better than Ghanian Jollof). I have been
fortunate enough to live in Nigeria for 4 years, and I would love to go again
some day.

I am currently a fourth year computer science student at the
University of Windsor, specializing in software engineering. I currently work with Telus as a sales associate.
I would probably choose to become a software developer and travel the world.
